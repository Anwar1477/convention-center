About Alpha Convention Center

Alpha Convention Center is one of the ORIGINAL and LEADING convention centers in Bangladesh. It was established in 2003 with the goal of providing the highest standard of services in the Hospitality Industry. Alpha Convention Centre offers Luxurious Banquet Halls, Business Conference Rooms, Business Centre, all of which that are equipped with a modern hygienic Kitchen, car parking facilities and a fantastic and cozy location in the central business district of Gulshan, Dhaka.
